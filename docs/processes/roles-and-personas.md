# Roles and Personas

Describe key roles referenced across process docs and their responsibilities.

## Project Sponsor

- **Owns outcomes:** approves major changes, budgets, and go/no-go decisions.
- **Escalation target:** receives critical and major risk escalations.
- **Communication:** receives steering committee updates and milestone-based stakeholder reports.

## Project Lead (Project Manager)

- **Coordinates delivery:** manages schedules, risks, and cross-team communication.
- **Runs ceremonies:** organizes standups, weekly syncs, and retrospectives.
- **Maintains artifacts:** keeps the project board, risk register, and status reports current.
- **Escalation owner:** first point of contact for all risk escalations; escalates major/critical issues to sponsor.

## Product Manager (PdM)

- **Defines outcomes:** prioritizes the backlog and measures success against goals.
- **Consulted on scope changes:** validates that changes align with product strategy.
- **Collaborates on planning:** partners with Project Lead to define milestones and Definition of Done.

## Developers

- **Implement features:** design and build changes with tests and documentation.
- **Collaborate on design:** participate in planning sessions and code reviews.
- **Raise blockers:** surface risks and blockers during standups and syncs.

## QA / Testing

- **Validates quality:** confirms acceptance criteria are met before release.
- **Runs smoke tests:** executes pre-release smoke tests and post-deploy verification.
- **Documents test results:** maintains test evidence for compliance and audit.

## Release Manager

- **Owns releases:** plans and approves the release window and deployment runbook.
- **Manages rollbacks:** identifies and tests rollback plans prior to deployment.
- **Post-release:** oversees post-release monitoring and incident response.

## Stakeholders

- **Provide inputs and approvals:** supply requirements, review deliverables, and approve releases.
- **Consulted on changes:** inform scope, priority, and communication plans.
- **Receive updates:** get regular status reports and stakeholder communications.
